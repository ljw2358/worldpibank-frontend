How will KYC be verified without a KYC screen?
Phased approach:

1) Lightweight checks: device reputation, IP risk, duplicate account heuristics, velocity & sanctions name screening.
2) Document + liveness (on-demand): only when risk triggers; privacy-first flow with a modular provider.
3) Periodic re-verification: risk-based cadence; encrypted storage; time-bounded access and retention.
What data is stored?
Minimum for compliance: hashed identifiers, risk scores, and audit logs. Sensitive docs encrypted at rest; least-privileged access.
